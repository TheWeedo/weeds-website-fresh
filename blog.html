Why We Grow All Organic (and Why You Should Care)

At Wellness by Weedo, how we grow is just as important as what we grow.

We don’t just say “organic” because it sounds nice—we live it. From seed to harvest, our cannabis is grown without synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or bottled nutrient shortcuts. Instead, we follow principles of Korean Natural Farming (KNF) and regenerative agriculture , crafting everything we feed our plants from scratch using local, natural inputs.

Why go to all that trouble?

Because cannabis is what scientists call a hyperaccumulator —a plant that absorbs and stores everything in its environment, including heavy metals, toxins, and pollutants. That means if it’s grown with chemical fertilizers, pesticides, or in contaminated soil, you’re not just smoking the plant—you’re smoking everything it absorbed .

We don’t mess with that.

We build our soil from the ground up—literally—using fermented plant juices (FPJ), indigenous microorganisms (IMO), natural minerals, and compost teas. These inputs bring life to the soil, not just nutrients. They help the plant grow stronger, more resilient, and more flavorful without needing synthetic boosters.

And when your flower is that clean, that vibrant, and that alive… you feel it.

There’s a deeper connection, a kind of harmony, that comes from working with nature instead of against it. It’s slower. It’s harder. But it’s real. And that’s what matters to us.

Whether you’re enjoying one of our rosin-pressed edibles, topicals, or just the flower itself—you’re experiencing pure, living cannabis grown with care, never compromise.

No shortcuts. No chemicals. Just good medicine.
