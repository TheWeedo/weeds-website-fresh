About Wellness by Weedo

We’re not a big team or a corporate brand. We’re two humans—John and Deb—doing what we love at the Lake of the Ozarks: growing powerful plants and crafting wellness products that actually help people feel better.

This isn’t just a business. It’s our lifestyle. We grow it, press it, infuse it, bake it, label it, and test it ourselves. Every product comes through our hands and reflects the care, curiosity, and intention we put into everything we do.

Behind the scenes are two soulmates who’ve spent years learning how to grow better, feel better, and live in harmony with the earth. You might know us as John and Deb. Or maybe... you’ve heard whispers about Weedo and Shroomina   (More on that soon.)

We know there are cheaper options out there—but that’s not what we’re about. We don’t cut corners, and we’re not trying to win a price war. We’re here to make the best products—not the most.

If you value potency, purity, and products made with intention—you’ll feel the difference. And if you know… you know.

We believe wellness should feel warm, welcoming, and maybe even a little wild. If you're into small-batch goodness, natural healing, and handcrafted cannabis—you're in the right place.
